About

A bit of background.

I started making servers when I was 13 just to play with friends, but it eventually became a serious hobby. Learned Linux, Java, and Docker along the way.

My biggest project was HearthCraft, a server that ran for 7 years and had over 40,000 players join. I handled the infrastructure and custom plugins, and worked alongside an amazing staff team to manage the community.

I'm used to dealing with dedicated servers, tracking down lag spikes, securing the backend, and configuring plugins so they actually fit what the players want.
